# Card Proposal Template

Structured template for proposing governed cards after intent is accepted.

## Goal

State the card outcome and scope anchor.

## Reality Model

State:
- `outer_vision`
- `current_solid_scope`
- `next_real_slice`
- `reality_state`
- `solidity_class`
- `proof_target`
- `non_goals`
- `illusion_risk`
- `promotion_gate`

## Constraints

List hard boundaries and human approval points.

## Acceptance

Define the machine-verifiable completion criteria.
